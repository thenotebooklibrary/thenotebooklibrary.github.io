BANANAMAN RETURNS

The Bananaman has returned. He is prowling the streets in hope of fresh clothes to ruin. But he passed a clothes store on the way. The Bananaman seems to like clothes worn, as to make the people wearing them feel angry about the bananas thrown at the person. He has been spotted throwing bananas at several civilians. These civilians ended up running away screaming. The Bananaman has been presumed a killing machine by police officers, and civilians. We ended up tracking him to the most desolate parts of the city, and cornered him behind a trash can. But then we spent days cleaning the bananas off our clothes. Hopefully he will be caught again.

---Lumater 2016
